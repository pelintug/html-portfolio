The Best Movies According to Pelin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Perfect Days

Hirayama cleans public toilets in Tokyo, lives his life in simplicity and daily tranquility. Some encounters also lead him to reflect on himself.

Little Miss Sunshine

A family determined to get their young daughter into the finals of a beauty pageant take a cross-country trip in their VW bus.

Fallen Angels

This Hong Kong-set crime drama follows the lives of a hitman, hoping to get out of the business, and his elusive female partner.
